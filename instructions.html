Assessment Instructions

Please read carefully before starting. The timer begins when you click Start Assessment.

⏱ Total Duration: 25 Minutes. The assessment auto-submits when time is over.
  • 🎮
    Game-Based Tasks — This is NOT a multiple-choice quiz. You will complete 8 interactive cognitive mini-games that measure different mental abilities.
  • ⏱
    Timed Assessment — You have 25 minutes total. Each game also has its own time limit shown on screen.
  • 🖱
    Interactions — You will click, drag, arrange blocks, remember sequences, and solve visual puzzles. No typing required for games.
  • 📱
    Mobile Friendly — The assessment works on mobile. Drag interactions use touch. Ensure your screen is not auto-rotating.
  • 🔒
    Tab Switching — Switching tabs or minimizing the browser will be recorded. Stay on this page throughout the assessment.
  • 💾
    Progress Saved — If you accidentally refresh, your progress is saved locally and you can resume if time remains.
  • 🌐
    Internet Required — Your results are submitted online at the end. Keep your internet connection active.
  • 🔇
    Quiet Environment — Find a calm, distraction-free space. This assessment measures focus and reaction speed.

8 Mini-Games You Will Play

🔷 Pattern Match
🧩 Block Arrangement
🧠 Memory Sequence
⚡ Quick Reaction
🔀 Rule Switching
◼ Puzzle Grid
🗺 Direction Quest
🔢 Calculation Sprint

Scoring

Scores are based on accuracy + speed + consistency. You earn points for correct answers, lose points for repeated mistakes, and get bonus points for fast, accurate responses.

Device Check: Make sure your device volume is not muted, your screen brightness is adequate, and you are on a stable connection before starting.

By starting, you confirm that you are the registered learner and will complete this assessment independently.
